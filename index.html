Standing since time forgot.

Our Unwritten Origins:

Hollow School has always stood at the edge of the town, settled in a place where the fog never seems to lift. Its halls echo with a strange stillness, as if something listens from behind the walls. Students often say the building feels older than the earth beneath it, older than anything that should still remain standing. Even in daylight, Hollow School carries a darkness that seems to breathe.

No one knows when it was built, and no one dares to ask why it never seems to age. Hollow School simply waits, as it always has.
